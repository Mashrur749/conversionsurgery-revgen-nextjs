# Execution Plan: 0 to $15K MRR

Solo founder. Day job. Calgary basement development contractors.
Goal: 15 paying clients. Timeline: 4-5 months.

**This plan tells you exactly what to do every day. No thinking required. Open it, do the thing, check it off.**

### When to call (and when NOT to)

| Time | What they&apos;re doing | Call? |
|------|----------------------|:-----:|
| Before 7:00am | Waking up, coffee | **NO** &mdash; too early, feels intrusive |
| 7:00-8:30am | Driving to site, loading truck | **YES** &mdash; best window, alone in truck, hands-free |
| 8:30am-4:00pm | Underground working, tools running | **NO** &mdash; won&apos;t answer, annoyed if they do |
| 12:00-12:30pm | Lunch break (some) | **DMs/texts only** &mdash; don&apos;t call, but messages land well |
| 4:00-5:30pm | Wrapping up, driving home | **OK but tired** &mdash; keep it short if you catch them |
| 6:00-7:00pm | Dinner with family | **NEVER** &mdash; they&apos;ll remember you negatively |
| 7:00-8:30pm | After dinner, relaxing | **YES** &mdash; second-best window, relaxed, phone in hand |
| After 8:30pm | Winding down | **Texts OK, no first calls** &mdash; they wake at 5:30am |

**Total: ~40-50 hours/week.** More than the Monte Carlo assumed (22-25). This compresses the timeline.

**Friday evenings: OFF.** Non-negotiable. Burnout prevention.

## Emergency Playbook

### &ldquo;I&apos;ve made 30 calls and zero connects&rdquo;
Switch to DMs for 3 days. Instagram DMs have 8% response rate with personalization. Also try the 6-7am morning slot &mdash; contractors answer before they&apos;re on site.

### &ldquo;I&apos;m getting connects but no demos&rdquo;
Your opener isn&apos;t landing. Try the instant demo approach: &ldquo;Can I show you something in 60 seconds? Call your own number right now.&rdquo; Skip the scheduling step entirely.

### &ldquo;I&apos;m doing demos but nobody is closing&rdquo;
Two likely causes: (1) the Revenue Leak Audit isn&apos;t landing &mdash; make it MORE specific to their business, or (2) you&apos;re not asking for the close. After the demo, say: &ldquo;Want to try the free month?&rdquo; and then stop talking. The silence is uncomfortable but necessary.

### &ldquo;I signed 3 clients but one just churned&rdquo;
Review the exit reason. If it&apos;s &ldquo;didn&apos;t see results&rdquo; &mdash; were you doing the strategy calls? Was their pipeline SMS showing activity? Fix the root cause. One early churn is normal &mdash; don&apos;t spiral. Replace them and move on.

### &ldquo;I&apos;m burning out at Week 6&rdquo;
Drop to 20 hrs/week for 2 weeks. Skip Sunday entirely. Only do calls Tuesday/Thursday/Saturday. Delivery can run on autopilot for 2 weeks &mdash; the platform handles it. Your energy is more important than your pipeline.

### &ldquo;I ran out of prospects&rdquo;
Expand to &ldquo;basement renovation Calgary&rdquo; and &ldquo;secondary suite Calgary&rdquo; search terms. Check HomeStars, Houzz, Kijiji. Ask Claude to help find more. If truly exhausted (unlikely at 150+ contractors), add kitchen/bath as a secondary niche.
