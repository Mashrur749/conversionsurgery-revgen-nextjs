## DELIVERY GUIDE

### Recommended: Share on screen during the demo call

Open with this before launching the demo:

> "Before we get into the demo, I did 10 minutes of research on your business this morning — can I show you what I found?"

Then screen-share this document. Walk through each section. Let the numbers land. Do not editorialize — just present what you found and let them react.

This does two things: (1) it establishes you as someone who does the work before asking for anything, and (2) it makes the rest of the demo about their specific situation, not a generic product overview.

**Pacing:** Spend 5–7 minutes on the audit before transitioning to the demo. If they start asking questions about how you fix it, that is a buying signal — slow down and go deeper.
