## 2. Service Description

ConversionSurgery provides a managed revenue recovery service for renovation contractors. The service covers lead response, estimate follow-up, appointment booking, payment collection, review generation, and past-client reactivation — operated and monitored by ConversionSurgery on Client's behalf.

Specifically, the service includes:

- **Near-Instant Lead Response** — Every inquiry (call, text, or web form) receives an automated response during permitted hours.
- **AI Conversation Agent** — An AI configured with Client's business information that qualifies leads, answers service questions, and books estimate appointments. Syncs with Google Calendar to prevent double-bookings.
- **Estimate Follow-Up** — Multi-touch follow-up sequences triggered when Client flags an estimate as sent.
- **Appointment Confirmation and No-Show Recovery** — Automated reminders before appointments; same-day follow-up if a lead does not appear.
- **Payment Collection** — Automated deposit and invoice reminders with payment links.
- **Review Generation** — Automated review request after each completed job with a direct link to Google.
- **Dormant Lead Reactivation** — Re-engagement of past inquiries and old estimates that went cold.
- **Dedicated Business Phone Number and Lead CRM** — A local phone number separate from Client's personal cell, and a dashboard showing every lead, conversation, and status.
- **Bi-Weekly Performance Reports** — A report every two weeks showing leads captured, response times, conversations handled, estimates followed up, appointments booked, and revenue impact.
- **Quarterly Growth Blitz** — A strategic campaign run every 90 days (dormant reactivation, review acceleration, pipeline building, or annual strategy review).

**What Client is responsible for:**
- One 30-minute onboarding call
- Flagging estimates as sent (by SMS keyword, reply, or dashboard — approximately 10 seconds each)
- Marking jobs as won or lost (approximately 30 seconds each)
- Responding when the AI escalates a conversation requiring human judgment

**What ConversionSurgery handles:**
AI setup and configuration, follow-up sequence management, compliance (CASL/CRTC), performance monitoring, message optimization, technical maintenance, bi-weekly reporting, and quarterly campaigns.

## 6. Performance Guarantees [PENDING COUNSEL REVIEW]

**Layer 1 — 30-Day Proof-of-Life Guarantee**

If Client does not receive at least five (5) Qualified Lead Engagements within the applicable proof window, Client is eligible for a refund of the first month service fee.

A Qualified Lead Engagement means: (a) an inbound lead received by the system, (b) a first automated system response timestamped in platform logs, and (c) at least one recipient reply after the system response.

**Layer 2 — 90-Day Revenue Recovery Guarantee**

If, by the end of the applicable recovery window, Client cannot identify at least one (1) Attributed Project Opportunity materially advanced by the Service, Client is eligible for a refund of the most recent monthly service fee and may request export of all captured lead and conversation records.

An Attributed Project Opportunity means: (a) a lead received an automated system response or follow-up action, (b) subsequent recorded engagement occurred (reply, booking, or resumed conversation), and (c) platform logs confirm system-initiated engagement before the opportunity progressed. If logs are inconclusive, ConversionSurgery will honor the refund.

**Volume Condition — Low-Volume Extension Formula** [PENDING COUNSEL REVIEW]

Both guarantees assume a baseline of fifteen (15) inbound inquiries per 30-day period. If actual inbound inquiry volume averages below 15 per 30-day period during the applicable guarantee window, guarantee timelines adjust proportionally:

> Adjusted Window = Base Window &times; (15 &divide; Actual Monthly Volume)

Actual Monthly Volume is the arithmetic average inbound inquiry count per 30-day period across the measured guarantee window. An inbound inquiry includes a missed call, form submission, or inbound SMS. Partial days round up to the next whole day. If average volume falls below 8 inquiries per month, ConversionSurgery will review the account individually.

## 7. Quiet Hours and Compliance [PENDING COUNSEL REVIEW]

ConversionSurgery enforces quiet-hours safeguards for outbound commercial messaging. System-initiated proactive outreach — including follow-up sequences, win-back campaigns, reactivation, review requests, and payment reminders — is queued for compliant delivery windows in accordance with CRTC telecom regulations (no commercial messages between 9 PM and 10 AM local time).

Direct conversational replies to a recipient's inbound inquiry are handled according to applicable law and ConversionSurgery's current compliance policy. ConversionSurgery is actively reviewing whether inbound-reply exemptions apply and will update this clause upon completion of that review.

Every message sent through the platform is compliant with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL): consent is tracked for every lead, opt-outs are honored immediately, and a full audit trail is maintained.
