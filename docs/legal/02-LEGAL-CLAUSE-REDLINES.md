## 1) Quiet-Hours Classification

### Draft Clause (Service Agreement)
"ConversionSurgery enforces quiet-hours safeguards for outbound commercial messaging. System-initiated proactive outreach (including follow-up sequences, win-back campaigns, reactivation, review requests, and payment reminders) is queued for compliant delivery windows. Direct conversational replies to a recipient's inbound inquiry are handled according to applicable law and ConversionSurgery's current compliance policy."

### Draft Public Claim Language (Until Legal Confirmation)
- Allowed:
  - "Near-instant response during compliant hours."
  - "24/7 system monitoring with compliant outbound delivery."
- Not allowed:
  - "30-second response 24/7" (without explicit legal qualifier)
  - "Always instant regardless of quiet hours"

### Counsel Decision Needed
- Is inbound-reply exemption valid under applicable rules?
- If yes: exact conditions and required disclosure text.
- If no: final compliant claim language for all written materials.

## 2) 30-Day + 90-Day Guarantee Language

### Draft Clause (Service Agreement)
"30-Day Proof-of-Life Guarantee: If Client does not receive at least five (5) Qualified Lead Engagements within the applicable proof window, Client is eligible for a refund of the first month service fee.

90-Day Revenue Recovery Guarantee: If, by the end of the applicable recovery window, Client cannot identify at least one (1) Attributed Project Opportunity materially advanced by the Service, Client is eligible for a refund of the most recent monthly service fee, and may request export of captured lead and conversation records.

Qualified Lead Engagement means: (a) inbound lead source, (b) first system response timestamped in platform logs, and (c) at least one recipient reply after system response.

Attributed Project Opportunity means: (a) lead received a system response or follow-up action, (b) subsequent recorded engagement occurred (reply, booking, or resumed conversation), and (c) Client confirms meaningful contribution by the Service to opportunity progression."

### Counsel Decision Needed
- Are these definitions sufficiently objective and enforceable?
- Required wording changes for consumer/commercial enforceability.
- Any required dispute/claims process language.

## 3) Low-Volume Extension Formula

### Draft Clause (Service Agreement)
"Guarantee windows assume a baseline of fifteen (15) inbound inquiries per 30-day period. If actual inbound inquiry volume averages below 15 per 30-day period during the applicable guarantee window, guarantee timelines adjust proportionally by:

Adjusted Window = Base Window x (15 / Actual Monthly Volume)

Actual Monthly Volume is calculated as the arithmetic average inbound inquiry count per 30-day period across the measured guarantee window."

### Draft Clarifications (for counsel to confirm)
- Round up partial days to next whole day.
- "Inbound inquiry" includes missed call, form submission, and inbound SMS.
- If average volume falls below 8/month, case-by-case review applies.

### Counsel Decision Needed
- Enforceability of formula and definitions.
- Required precision/rounding language.
- Required fallback language for very low-volume scenarios.

## 4) Unlimited Messaging Claim + Contract Qualifier

### Draft Clause (Service Agreement)
"Unlimited lead conversations and automated messaging are included for normal, in-scope renovation business use under this Agreement. Excluded uses include mass broadcasting, personal/non-business messaging, and use for services or entities outside contracted scope. ConversionSurgery may review materially abnormal usage patterns to ensure platform integrity and compliance."

### Draft Sales Language
- Allowed headline: "Unlimited. No caps. No overages."
- Required contract backstop: include clause above in signed agreement.

### Counsel Decision Needed
- Is the claim+qualifier structure compliant and non-misleading?
- Any required wording to reduce false-advertising or unfair-practice exposure.
